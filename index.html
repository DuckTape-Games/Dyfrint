DYFRINT was created by cousins Chris and Connor Herriman and Chris's brother Bryan Reeves one summer while on a family vacation to South Carolina. The idea behind the brand is to promote individualism and being yourself.

DYFRINT launched their pilot design, a black hoodie that says "Don't Be Afraid to be You Everyone Else is Taken" on the back and has the brand logo and UBU embroidered on the chest and right wrist respectively, on November 12th 2024
